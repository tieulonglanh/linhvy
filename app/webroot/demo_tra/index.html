The Tea Company with a Difference

At Clipper, we’ve been making magical teas in Dorset since 1984. From small beginnings in a Dorset kitchen, we are now the 6th biggest tea company in the UK. We believe that every cup of tea is important, so we strive to deliver the best tasting tea, using the highest quality ingredients, always ethically sourced.
